About Us

ABN was registered in 2024 by the NGO Board to serve vulnerable children in Kilifi. After USAID’s exit in 2025, many orphans and children living with HIV were left without support. ABN stepped in to provide food, treatment support, and psychosocial care to over 4,720 children, including 771 living with HIV and 122 exposed infants.

Our Work

  • Food baskets
  • Transport to HIV care
  • Medical bills
  • Psychosocial support
  • School materials
  • Menstrual hygiene & WASH
